About BBS

Bharatiya Bhasha Samiti: A High Powered Committee for Promotion of Indian Languages has been constituted by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, vide letter no.8-46/2021/L-II, dated 15th November, 2021. The mandate of this committee would be to explore and recommend pathways of the holistic and multi-disciplinary growth of Indian languages as envisaged in the National Education Policy-2020.

chairman's message

Shri Chamu Krishna Shastry is the Chairman of Bhararatiya Bhasha Samiti. He is a renound educationalist who had been working continuously for promotion of Indian languages. His early works were the promotion of sanskrit language, development of modern pedagogical techniques to enhance Sanskrit communicational skills of people and making Sanskrit a vibrant communicative language. He received Padmashree award form Govt. Of India on 2017 for its life long contribution to the nation. He has rendered his service as Senior Consultant Languages in MHRD.
